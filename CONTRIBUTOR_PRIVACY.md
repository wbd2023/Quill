# Quill Contributor Privacy Notice

Effective date: 20 July 2026

This notice explains how William Ba Duong, as the maintainer of Quill, handles personal
information associated with the contributor licence agreement process.

## Information handled

The CLA process may handle:

- your authenticated GitHub account and account identifier;
- your GitHub-associated email address;
- the full legal name that you enter in the signing form;
- your responses to required contributor declarations;
- the CLA version or Gist revision that you accepted;
- the date and time of acceptance; and
- the pull requests and Contributions associated with your GitHub account.

## Purposes

This information is used to:

- verify and administer CLA acceptance;
- associate accepted Contributions with the relevant rights grant;
- maintain the Project's intellectual-property history;
- respond to licensing, ownership, or legal questions;
- support commercial licensing, project transfer, or due diligence; and
- protect or enforce rights relating to the Project.

## Services and storage

The standard signing process uses [CLA Assistant][cla-assistant], a service operated by SAP, and
GitHub. CLA Assistant authenticates signers through GitHub and stores signer records. Its public
documentation states that hosted signer data is stored in an Azure Cosmos DB in Europe and can be
exported by repository owners.

The Project Owner may download signer exports and store them in a private repository or other
private backup. These private records are not intended for publication.

Review the [CLA Assistant privacy statement][cla-privacy] and
[GitHub Privacy Statement][github-privacy] before signing.

## Public information

The Project Owner does not require your full legal name to be posted in the public repository or
on a pull request. Your public GitHub username, commits, pull requests, comments, and other
information that you choose to publish remain public under GitHub's terms.

## Retention

Acceptance records may be retained while an Accepted Contribution remains in the Project and for
as long as reasonably necessary to establish, exercise, transfer, or defend rights relating to the
Project.

## Access, correction, and enquiries

Use a private contact method listed on the Project Owner's GitHub profile. If no private method is
listed, open an issue at <https://github.com/wbd2023/Quill/issues> asking for a private
contact channel,
without including personal information in the issue.
