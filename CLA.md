**Copyright Rights** means copyright and analogous rights protecting works of authorship,

**Contribution** means original software, source code, documentation, tests, configuration,
designs, or other copyright material that You intentionally submit for possible inclusion in the
Project and in which You own or control the rights needed to grant this Agreement. A Contribution
includes additions to or modifications of existing Project material.

- material conspicuously marked `Not a Contribution`;

**Submit** means intentionally providing material for possible inclusion in the Project through a
pull request, commit, patch, Project-managed source-control system, or another written channel
expressly designated by the Project Owner.

**Accepted Contribution** means the part of a Contribution that the Project Owner merges,
cherry-picks, copies into a Project-controlled repository, or expressly accepts in writing for
inclusion in the Project.

**Third-Party Material** means material in a submission for which You do not own or control the
rights needed to grant this Agreement.

**Permitted Successor** means a person or legal entity that acquires all or substantially all of
the Project, or the business principally relating to the Project, and assumes this Agreement under
Section 13.
